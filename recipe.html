Chocolate Chip Cookies
Baking temperature: 375°F
Time required: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup Granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 cup Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 packet (12 oz) Semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix sugars, butter, vanilla, and eggs in a large bowl
  3. Stir in chocolate chips
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt (dough will be stiff)
  5. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned (centers will be soft)
  7. Cool slightly, then remove from the cookie sheet
  8. Cool on a wire rack
